ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Christian Barlow, a 20-year-old guy from Castlegar, BC. I was born in Seattle, WA, USA.

I picked up a hockey stick when I was 2, and played up until a couple years ago. Over the years, I would train by myself and with my team doing dryland, on-ice, and gym exercises.

The ability to stay in good mental and physical shape for so long is very hard, but it is required for such a vigorous sport.

A few years ago, I started making music and DJing at parties, as sort of my substitute for hockey. I have played all over the West Kootenays, and even opened for sold out shows in various spots of Washington.

These concerts are amazing, because I am able to meet the headliners, who are often people I have looked up to since a kid.

Musically, I enjoy anything from country to classic rock to face melting death metal depending on my mood.
